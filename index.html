Hi! My name's Heather Payne. I'm a 26-year-old entrepreneur living in Toronto, Canada. I'm the founder of HackerYou and Ladies Learning Code. I'm also an angel investor (with one exit so far), one of Canada's 100 Most Powerful Women, and I love being a role model for people who are thinking about changing their careers and their lives.

I'm really passionate about showing people that they can do anything they put their mind to, whether it's learning to code, starting a business, investing in a startup, or taking on some other exciting challenge. If I can do it, you can, too.
